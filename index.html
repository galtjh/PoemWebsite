Daughter's Day Delight

In quiet eve's soft, golden glow,
At Mukherjee Nagar, we often go,
My daughter's talents brightly show,
In evening walks and code she's sown

She dribbles, shoots, in basketball's court,
With Scratch, she codes, a wondrous chase,
Her self-study blooms, like a rose.
In the evening's clam, her curiosity flows.

In nature's grace, we often roam,
Quality time, a cherished home,
These moments together, hearts unite,
Daughter's day, in love's pure delight.
